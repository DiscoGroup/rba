A Considered Fit

Paid Media Is a Powerful Lever
— In the Right Hands

Paid advertising can be transformative for the right firm with the right offer and the right intake. It can also quietly drain budget for the wrong fit. A candid look at where this work tends to belong.

A Good Fit If
  • You have a clear, defined ideal client — someone you’d be willing to invest $200–$1,500 to acquire as a consultation.
  • Your intake process can handle a 30–100% increase in inbound leads within 60 days without dropping the ball on follow-up.
  • You can articulate — or are willing to develop — a real differentiated offer. Generic “free consultation” offers don’t convert anymore.
  • You’re willing to invest at least $3,000/month in ad spend on top of management. Below that, paid social won’t out-perform organic effort.
Perhaps Not Yet If
  • You’d like a guarantee of leads. No reputable agency can guarantee outcomes from paid media — platforms shift constantly. What we can guarantee is rigorous process, transparent reporting, and the right to walk away at any time.
  • Your intake currently responds to inquiries within a day or two. Paid leads cool quickly, so we’d ask you to tune intake first — happy to help you do that before any spend.
  • You’re hoping paid media will permanently replace organic effort. Paid amplifies the credibility you already have — it doesn’t create it from nothing.

Here’s Exactly How This Works

A Built System — Not a Guess

Every Raise The Bar paid engagement follows the same sequence. No experiments, no “let’s see what happens.”

  1. 01

    Offer & Funnel Audit (Week 1)

    Before we spend a dollar, we audit your offer, your landing page, your intake speed, and your follow-up sequences. If anything will leak the leads we generate, we fix it first — together — before ads go live.

  2. 02

    Creative & Copy Production (Week 2)

    We produce 8–12 ad variants per campaign: short-form videos, static graphics, story-format creatives, and platform-native copy. Every asset reviewed and approved by you before it touches a platform.

  3. 03

    Campaign Build & Launch (Week 3)

    Audience targeting, conversion tracking, retargeting layers, ad-account hygiene, platform-policy compliance — all configured to a documented internal checklist. We document every decision so you (or any future agency) can audit it.

  4. 04

    Daily Optimisation (Ongoing)

    Campaigns are reviewed and optimised every working day. Winning creative scales. Losing creative pauses. Audiences refresh on a defined cadence. You don’t see the work — you see the results.

  5. 05

    Weekly Performance Reporting

    Every Monday: spend, cost-per-lead, lead quality scoring, recommended changes. Plus a 30-minute monthly strategy call so the campaigns evolve with your business — not against it.
