Social Media for Law Firms
Built to Be Known
Before They’re Needed

By the time someone realises they need a lawyer, the decision is already made — consciously or otherwise. Our job is to make sure your firm is the one they’ve already been watching.

A Considered Fit

A Long-Game Partnership,
Not a Posting Service

The social media work we do compounds over months, not weeks. A few honest notes on who this is built for — and the kinds of firms who are usually better served elsewhere.

A Good Fit If
  • You understand that authority on social media takes 90–180 days to compound — and you’re prepared to invest before the inbox starts ringing.
  • You (or one of your attorneys) are willing to spend 30 minutes a month on camera or in a strategy call. The best social presences are voiced, not ghost-written.
  • You want your firm to feel like a known quantity to a prospect before the first call — not a name they have to research.
  • You’re tired of watching less-qualified firms get the attention you should be getting because they’ve been posting consistently and you haven’t.
Perhaps Not Yet If
  • Your goal is viral reach rather than visible authority. We build for credibility and longevity, not the dopamine of a single breakout post.
  • You’re looking for a single piece of content to produce a single retained client. Social media is a trust engine — it compounds across many touchpoints, not one.
  • You’d prefer a fully faceless, attorney-anonymous account. We can do that, but we’ll be candid that personal-brand presence consistently outperforms it, so a different agency may be a better match.
  • You’d like us to make the final ethics call on each post. We’ll always flag concerns and propose alternatives — but the final word belongs with you and your bar counsel, every time.

Who You Become

The Firm That Owns the Room
Before Walking Into It

We don’t turn attorneys into influencers. We turn them into the recognisable authority in their practice area — the firm whose name shows up before the search ever starts.

  • You become the firm prospects already feel they know.

    By consultation #1, they’ve seen you explain three concepts, share two opinions, and answer a question that matched their exact situation. The sales conversation becomes a logistics conversation.

  • Your firm becomes the one referring attorneys send their overflow to.

    Other lawyers watch other lawyers’ content. When your perspective is visible, you become the obvious referral — not because you asked, but because they’ve seen you do the work.

  • You become the firm that recruits passive candidates.

    The top associates aren’t scrolling job boards. They’re scrolling LinkedIn. A firm with a sharp, consistent presence attracts the candidates who’d never have applied through a posting.

  • Your firm becomes the one your local press calls first.

    When journalists need a quote on a new ruling or trending case, they reach for whoever’s most visible — and most quoted lawyers are visible because they’ve been posting for two years, not because they got lucky.

Here’s Exactly How This Works

A Repeatable Engine,
Tuned to Your Firm’s Voice

Our monthly process produces consistent, on-brand content across every platform you choose to be on — without consuming your week.

  1. 01

    Voice & Strategy Onboarding (Weeks 1–2)

    A 90-minute voice-capture session with the attorney(s) we’re building around. We map your perspectives, opinions, recurring questions you hear, and the topics you’d be quoted on. This becomes your editorial spine.

  2. 02

    Monthly Content Sprint (Day 1–5 of Every Month)

    We deliver a content calendar covering 12–20 pieces per month: short-form videos, branded graphics, written posts, and platform-specific variations. You review, approve, or redirect — all in one batch, all from your phone.

  3. 03

    Production & Scheduling (Day 6–15)

    Our team produces every asset — editing video, designing graphics, writing captions, researching hashtags, and scheduling delivery at the platform-optimal times. You see nothing until it’s ready.

  4. 04

    Engagement & Conversation Layer (Daily)

    Comments answered within four working hours in your firm’s voice. DMs triaged and forwarded with context. Mentions logged. Saved posts tracked. The conversations a great social presence sparks become a competitive moat.

  5. 05

    Monthly Performance Review (End of Month)

    A written report covering reach, saves, profile visits, link clicks, and inbound leads attributed to social. Plus three specific recommendations for the next month based on what’s actually working — not generic best practices.
