COO & Co-Founder

Meghan Palaia

CEO & Co-Founder of Raise The Bar Agency — helping law firms move from obscurity to authority through precision marketing and brand strategy.

The Voice Behind the Strategy

Meghan Palaia is the Co-Founder and Chief Operating Officer of Raise The Bar Agency. She oversees client communications, content development, project deliverables, and the operational systems that keep the agency running smoothly. Working closely with CEO and Co-Founder Agata Reilly, Meghan turns strategy into action — ensuring that every project is executed with consistency, precision, and attention to detail.

Her expertise lies in helping attorneys identify and communicate what makes their firms exceptional: translating years of experience, results, and professional accomplishment into messaging that reinforces their credibility and reflects the reputation they have worked so hard to earn.

Fifteen Years in Legal Marketing

Meghan brings more than 15 years of experience creating content almost exclusively for law firms. That depth gives her an understanding of the challenges attorneys face, the standards they uphold, and the level of professionalism required to represent them effectively — an understanding that goes far beyond the surface of any brief.

She knows that attorneys are not simply looking for content. They are looking for a partner who understands the weight of what they do and the trust their clients place in them. That knowledge shapes every sentence she writes.

The Science Behind Credibility

At the heart of Meghan’s work is a deep interest in what drives trust, credibility, and human connection. She is particularly passionate about helping attorneys understand how their expertise is perceived online — and what factors influence that perception.

Her work draws on principles of behavioral science, authority psychology, and brand voice development. She closely follows emerging technologies reshaping how professionals build visibility and connect with their audiences, bringing that knowledge directly into the strategies she builds for clients.

Education & the Written Word

A lifelong writer with a deep appreciation for language, Meghan holds dual bachelor’s degrees in English and Journalism/Public Relations, a minor in French, and a master’s degree in English. Her background in writing, storytelling, and communication shapes the way she approaches every engagement — and fuels her passion for helping attorneys build trust, establish authority, and connect with the people they serve.

She believes that behind every great law firm is a human story worth telling — and that the reputations attorneys spend decades building should be reflected as powerfully online as they are in the real world.

Beyond the Office

Outside Raise The Bar Agency, Meghan is a wife, mom, avid reader, travel enthusiast, gardener, lifelong soccer player, and devoted dog lover. She brings the same care and commitment to everything she loves — and that orientation toward excellence is evident in every piece of work she produces.

15 Years. One Industry. Zero Shortcuts.

Meghan has spent her entire career writing almost exclusively for law firms — which means she understands the standards attorneys hold themselves to, the trust their clients place in them, and the professionalism required to represent them well. She does not write generic content and apply it to legal. She writes legal, and she has for over a decade and a half.
