CEO & Co-Founder

Agata Reilly

CEO & Co-Founder of Raise The Bar Agency — helping law firms move from obscurity to authority through precision marketing and brand strategy.

Creative Direction for Reputation-Driven Brands

With 10+ years of digital marketing experience, Agata helps law firms and professional service brands translate real-world credibility into a digital presence that reflects the caliber of their work. Her background includes video marketing, brand strategy, social media, paid advertising, SEO, content marketing, graphic design, and website design.

At Raise The Bar Agency, Agata oversees the creative direction behind each client’s brand presence, from messaging and visual identity to content strategy, video concepts, campaign direction, and platform-specific execution. Her role is to make sure every piece of content feels intentional, professional, and aligned with the reputation a firm has worked hard to build. She is especially passionate about video marketing and brand storytelling, creating content that communicates a brand’s personality, speaks directly to the needs of its ideal clients, and performs with purpose across social media, websites, commercials, and paid advertising campaigns.

A Foundation in Legal Marketing

Agata’s digital marketing career began in London, where she worked at a digital marketing agency serving national and international brands. That experience gave her a strong foundation in campaign strategy, creative execution, and building brands across competitive markets.

She later founded her own London-based agency, Outrank Media, after her younger brother was diagnosed with bone cancer at just nine years old. Remote work was not yet the standard, so Agata created her own path, building an agency that allowed her to continue serving clients while being present for her family. Through Outrank Media, she provided SEO, content marketing, and website design for law firms, helping legal clients strengthen their visibility and communicate their expertise online with greater clarity. Her brother is healthy today, and that chapter remains a defining example of Agata’s resilience, faith, and ability to build under pressure.

Staying Ahead of How People Pay Attention

Agata continues to invest in advanced marketing education and professional communities that keep her work sharp, current, and platform-aware. She is a member of One Peak Creative’s viral video community, where she studies short-form video strategy, audience retention, hooks, and content formats designed for today’s social platforms.

Her professional development also includes Social Media Examiner’s Social Media Marketing Society, which she joined in 2024, and AI Business Society, which she joined in 2025. She also attended Social Media Marketing World in person in both 2025 and 2026, continuing to study the evolving intersection of social media, AI, content strategy, and business growth.

Work Ethic Built From the Ground Up

Originally from Poland, Agata moved to London at 19 with two suitcases and the determination to build a life from the ground up. While studying full time at Roehampton University, she also worked full time in London’s hospitality industry, gaining experience across refined English establishments and Michelin-starred restaurants before growing into restaurant management. Fun fact: she is also a certified mixologist, with experience crafting cocktails and designing cocktail menus for London bars.

That season shaped her work ethic, resilience, emotional intelligence, and attention to detail. One of her London managers described her as “conscientious and hard working,” noting that she went “above and beyond” from the beginning and already had “a real passion for marketing.”

Education, Perspective, and Professional Standard

Agata graduated from Roehampton University with First-Class Honours in Business Management and Digital Media, an academic distinction comparable to summa cum laude in the United States. Her professors described her as “a creative and critical thinker” and “diligent and reliable,” recognizing her leadership, time management, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills.

Her path across countries, industries, and cultures has shaped her adaptability, emotional intelligence, and ability to build brands with both strategy and human depth.

Beyond the Agency

Outside of work, Agata is a wife, a proud dog mom to her boxer, Lola, and a lover of photography, hiking in the Sierra Nevada and Tahoe area, gardening, and baking. Her Christian faith is an important part of her life. Her favorite Bible verse is Philippians 4:13: “I can do all things through Christ who strengthens me,” a promise she carries into both her personal and professional life.

Agata Reilly is the CEO and Co-Founder of Raise The Bar Agency, where she leads creative direction, brand strategy, client experience, and the visual standard behind the agency's work. For Agata, great marketing is about representing what is already true with clarity, strategy, and authority. She stands beside attorneys and firm leaders as a creative partner who understands that reputation is not something to gamble with. It is something to protect, elevate, and make impossible to overlook.
