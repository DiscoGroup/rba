Our Story

Meet the Team Behind
Raise The Bar

Two founders. One mission: helping law firms bridge the gap between real-world credibility and online authority.

CEO & Co-Founder

Agata Reilly

Agata brings a creative director’s eye and a strategist’s mind to every client partnership at Raise The Bar Agency. With a background in branding, visual identity, and strategic positioning, she helps law firms build a digital presence that reflects the caliber of their work and the reputation they’ve worked years to earn.

She co-founded Raise The Bar Agency after seeing too many exceptional attorneys struggle with the same problem: their expertise was undeniable, but their visibility failed to reflect it. While less-qualified competitors captured attention through stronger marketing and a more visible online presence, highly respected firms were often overlooked. She built this agency to help law firms close that gap — ensuring the firms that deserve recognition are the ones being seen.

COO & Co-Founder

Meghan Palaia

Meghan is fascinated by what drives trust, credibility, and decision-making — and how those factors influence the way prospective clients choose a law firm. With more than 15 years of experience creating content for law firms, she helps attorneys communicate their expertise, strengthen their credibility, and connect with prospective clients in a way that feels authentic and trustworthy.

She co-founded Raise The Bar Agency after seeing too many accomplished attorneys struggle to articulate what truly set them apart. Their expertise was evident, but their messaging often failed to reflect the depth of their experience, results, and professional reputation. Drawing on principles of behavioral science, authority psychology, and strategic messaging, she helps law firms translate those strengths into content that builds trust, reinforces credibility, and resonates with the clients they serve.

Our Mission

We Help Great Lawyers Get the
Recognition They Deserve Online

Our mission is to help law firms bridge the gap between real-world credibility and online visibility. Whether guiding established practices ready to extend their authority online or helping new firms lay the foundation for a lasting legacy, we redefine how law firms tell their story. You’ve earned trust in the courtroom — we help you build it online.

Our Vision

Elevating the Standard of
Legal Marketing

Our vision is to elevate the standard of legal marketing — transforming how law firms build trust, visibility, and recognition in a digital world. We aim to set a new benchmark for excellence, where every firm’s online presence mirrors the integrity and impact of its real-world reputation.

We aspire to help our clients stand out in the legal industry with authenticity and confidence — helping lawyers build a brand where credibility is visible, trust is tangible, and every story is told with clarity and purpose.

Our Values

The Principles Behind Every Engagement

  • Trust Is Our Foundation

    We believe trust is the cornerstone of every strong brand. We earn it through reliability and follow-through — and we help our clients build it online, crafting brands that are credible, consistent, and worthy of confidence.

  • Partnership Over Transaction

    We don't see clients — we see partners. We build long-term, trust-based relationships grounded in communication, respect, and shared vision. We invest deeply in understanding you and your practice — so together, we can create a brand that truly represents who you are.

  • Where Strategy Meets Craftsmanship

    Every brand we build is crafted with purpose, precision, and pride. We treat marketing as an art form, where strategy and creativity work in harmony — designed with discipline and driven by imagination.

  • Integrity in Action

    We take the same pride in building your brand as you do in building your practice — every detail guided by integrity and purpose. We do what we say, deliver what we promise, and stand behind every detail with pride and accountability.

  • Excellence as Standard

    To raise the bar for our clients, we must first raise it for ourselves. Excellence is not a goal — it's our baseline, measured by the care in every detail, the precision in every decision, and the pride in every execution.

  • Clarity Builds Confidence

    Clarity drives everything we do — from strategy to delivery. We define expectations up front, communicate with intention, and maintain full transparency throughout our process.

  • Boutique Approach. World-Class Results.

    Our boutique structure gives us the freedom to immerse ourselves in every detail — combining personal attention with strategic precision. Quality isn't a function of size; it's a result of focus.
